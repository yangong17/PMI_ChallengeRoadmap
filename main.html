Executive Summary

Project: PMI-LA Summer Case Competition Challenge

Timeline: August 5, 2025 - October 25, 2025 (12 weeks total)

Culmination: Professional Development Day (PDD) Final Presentations

Project Overview

The PMI-LA Project Management Challenge is a comprehensive 12-week initiative designed to engage project management professionals and students in a real-world case competition. The challenge combines academic rigor with practical application, culminating in final presentations at the October 25, 2025 Professional Development Day.

Key Objectives

  • Enhance PMI-LA's visibility and member engagement
  • Strengthen partnerships with UCLA and USC
  • Provide hands-on PM experience through case study methodology
  • Create networking opportunities for participants
  • Showcase innovative project management solutions

Critical Success Factors

  • Timely completion of all project phases
  • Quality engagement from academic partners
  • Effective marketing and registration campaign
  • Seamless technology platform implementation
  • Professional presentation venue and logistics
